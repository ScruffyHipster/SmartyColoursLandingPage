Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

SmartyColours ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the SmartyColours app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SmartyColours is designed for children and families. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users, including children.

  • No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • No location data
  • No user-generated content is uploaded to our servers

2. Children’s Privacy

The app complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not knowingly collect or store any personal data from children under 13. Parents can use the app confidently knowing their child’s data is not being tracked or stored.

3. Local Data Storage

Any drawings or preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third-party service.

4. In-App Purchases

SmartyColours offers optional in-app purchases (IAP) to unlock additional coloring packs. Payments are processed securely through the App Store and we do not have access to your payment information.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-focused, anonymized analytics to understand app performance (e.g., how many times a coloring page is opened). This data is never linked to any individual and is only used to improve the app experience.
