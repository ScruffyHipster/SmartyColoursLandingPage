Is SmartyColours safe for my child?

Absolutely. There are no in-app ads, and the interface is designed for young children with no external links or purchases visible to them.

Do I need an Apple Pencil?

No! Kids can use their fingers or any stylus. Apple Pencil adds precision, but it's not required.
